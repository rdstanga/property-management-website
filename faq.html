Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you accept pets?

A: Simply put, no. Pets are a common reason for expensive damage to homes. They also pose a risk to people other than the pet's owner entering the property, even after the tenant and pet have vacated the property (allergic reactions to fur/dander and to those with compromised breathing).

Q: Who is responsible for utilities (electricity, water, gas, internet, trash)?

A: The tenant is responsible for all utilities unless otherwise stipulated.

Q: Do you accept tenants on assistance programs?

A: Yes.

Q: If a property is vacant and we are in the process of repairing it, will Stanga & Sons hold the property for me if I make a down payment?

A: We do not accept payments until we officially list the property. Stanga & Sons will place you on a list of interested parties, first come first serve.

Q: How do I apply?

A: Click this link and fill out the form.

Q: May I have trampolines, water/slip & slides, swingsets, or other outdoor playgym equipment in the yard?

A: Unfortunately, this is not allowed due to the liability it poses.

Q: Who is responsible for lawn maintenance?

A: The tenant is responsible for lawn care. Stanga & Sons can often provide grass cutting services, but we will charge a fee.
