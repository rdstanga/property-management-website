Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you accept pets?

A: No, no, and no again. Did we mention no?

Q: Do you accept smoking on the property?

A: Smoking of any kind is not permitted.

Q: Who is responsible for utilities (electricity, water, gas, internet, and trash)?

A: The tenant is responsible for all utilities unless otherwise stipulated.

Q: Do you accept tenants on assistance programs?

A: Yes.

Q: If a property is vacant and being repaired, will Stanga & Sons hold the property for me if I make a down payment?

A: We do not accept deposits until we sign a lease for the property, and we do not sign leases until the property is move-in ready.

Q: How do I apply?

A: Click this link and fill out the form.

Q: May I have trampolines, water/slip & slides, swingsets, or other outdoor playgym equipment in the yard?

A: Unfortunately, these are not allowed due to the liability issues they pose.

Q: Who is responsible for lawn maintenance?

A: The tenant is responsible for lawn care. Stanga & Sons can often provide grass cutting services, but we will charge a fee (subject to variance based on yard size).

Q: May I use the property to operate a small business?

A: Our residential properties are for residential use only. We do have an office with available space to use for commerical purposes. Please inquire if interested.
